8x8x8 LED Cube

August 5, 2016

For my personal entertainment, I built an 8x8x8 LED cube. It was great soldering practice too.

The cube features 512 individual blue LEDs controlled using an STC 40-pin microcontroller and 8 latches. The LED cube was built on a purchased printed circuit board and required about 1,300 solder joints. It’s powered by USB and even programmable via UART.
