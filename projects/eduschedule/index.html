EduSchedule

May 20, 2019

EduSchedule is a web app that helps students and teachers manage appointments. It was created as a self-directed project during my final high school engineering class and was intended to provide a more efficient method of scheduling meetings with school faculty.

Building EduSchedule helped me learn Django, a great framework for quickly prototyping web apps and APIs.
