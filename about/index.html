About Me

Hi, I’m Michael - an aspiring computer scientist, engineer, researcher, and technologist. Currently, I am studying computer science at Northwestern University. For years, I have been passionate about digital technology and have a special interest in information security and privacy, systems software, networking, and embedded devices / robotics. For leisure, I enjoy music, cycling, and occasionally climbing.

As a programmer, I value robust and pragmatic design and the Unix philosophy, as well as open source development. As such, some of my completed projects are published on GitHub.

I am always open to interesting work on open source software and job opportunities. Feel free to say hello!
